History

Adventure Rapids Expeditions was born out of a shared love for nature and the thrill of navigating wild waters. Founded in the scenic town of Riverside in 1995, our journey began with a single raft, a passionate group of friends, and a vision to introduce others to the exhilaration of white-water rafting. As we look ahead, our commitment to delivering exceptional white-water rafting adventures remains as strong as ever.

White-Water Rafting Limited

Mission:To provide thrilling and memorable outdoor adventures through white-water rafting while fostering a deeper and safer connection with nature.

History

Adventure Rapids Expeditions was born out of a shared love for nature and the thrill of navigating wild waters. Founded in the scenic town of Riverside in 1995, our journey began with a single raft, a passionate group of friends, and a vision to introduce others to the exhilaration of white-water rafting. As we look ahead, our commitment to delivering exceptional white-water rafting adventures remains as strong as ever. We continue to explore new river routes, introduce innovative safety measures, and broaden our educational programs.

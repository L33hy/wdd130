Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is the most populous country on the African continent and is known for its rich cultural diversity, with over 250 distinct ethnic groups and languages. Its capital city is Abuja, while Lagos serves as its largest and most economically vibrant city. Nigeria boasts a significant role in Africa's political and economic landscape, with an economy driven by oil production, agriculture, and a burgeoning tech industry. However, the country also faces social and economic challenges, including issues related to governance, infrastructure, and healthcare. Despite these challenges, Nigeria's vibrant music, art, and literature scenes contribute to its dynamic cultural heritage and global influence.
